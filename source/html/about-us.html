About Zubizi Web Solutions

At Zubizi Web Solutions, we are dedicated to empowering businesses by simplifying complex operations through innovative software solutions. Established in 2017, we've grown to become a trusted partner for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across India, especially in industries like retail, textile, and garment manufacturing. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make business processes easier, faster, and more efficient for SMEs.

Through our relentless commitment to innovation, customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement, we have become one of the leading IT companies in India, delivering practical, efficient, and cutting-edge software solutions to help businesses thrive in today's competitive digital landscape.

Our Mission

At Zubizi, our mission is to simplify business operations for SMEs and drive growth by providing software solutions that reduce operational complexity. We understand the challenges that small and medium-sized businesses face, especially in managing their accounting, compliance, and operational needs. By developing smart, intuitive, and easy-to-use solutions, we aim to empower business owners and managers to make informed, data-driven decisions without the burden of manual processes or outdated systems.

Our goal is to help SMEs focus on what truly matters: growing their businesses. We believe that by reducing administrative tasks and automating key business functions, we allow our clients to concentrate on scaling their operations, improving their services, and delivering exceptional value to their customers.

Zubizi Web Solutions is an Indian IT company headquartered in Kolkata, West Bengal. Our team comprises highly skilled and experienced professionals who bring years of expertise in software development, IT solutions, and customer service. Since our founding, we have worked with a growing number of businesses, earning the trust of over 1,000 clients across various sectors.
